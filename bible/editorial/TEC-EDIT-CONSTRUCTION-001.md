# Technique: Construction form

Binds to: synthesis (elenchus move 3, the answer earned).
Archetype bridge: ratio/specimen (modular grid construction, grid must remain visible).

What goes inside: sometimes a diagram, sometimes a typographic event built letter by letter on a visible grid (like the Hofmann exposition blob), sometimes a chart, sometimes a constructed sigil. The choice is content-driven. The constraint is that the construction must show its work.

Reference images:
- Hofmann construction grid (the grid underneath, the premise)
- Hofmann exposition poster (the figure derived from it, the conclusion)
Hold these side by side to see what the construction form means.
