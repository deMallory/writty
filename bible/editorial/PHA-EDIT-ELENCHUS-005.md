# Phase 5: Refuse to design

Non-retrievable structural node. Surfaces via CONTAINS edge from PBK-EDIT-ELENCHUS-001.

## Advance criterion

Phases 1-4 are complete. Three sentences are clear. Four form-assignments are stated. Only then does design begin.

## The refusal condition

If the three sentences will not clarify, do not proceed. The article is not ready. A site of three real investigations is worth more than a site of thirty wandering essays.
